About the Platform

Nagrik Aur Samvidhan aims to spread constitutional literacy in an engaging way by simplifying legal concepts for all citizens, including children and youth.

Our games make learning about the Constitution of India fun and accessible, promoting inclusivity and understanding of fundamental rights and duties.
